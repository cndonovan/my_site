Given an array A of n numbers, produce an array B such that B[i] is the product of A[0], A[1], ... A[i-1], A[i+1], ... A[n]. In other words, B[i] is the product of all entries in A except for A[i].
What happens if A includes a single zero?
Multiple zeroes?
Negative numbers?
What if A is empty?
What if the number in A are "large"? What kinds of errors will happen in different languages?
What if you cannot use the division operator?
